Related Gases  

INDUSTRIAL GASES FOR THE METAL PRODUCTION INDUSTRY

  • Share
  • Print

Oxygen

Oxygen is a component of metal processing, and is used to replace or enrich air, ultimately increasing combustion efficiency in both ferrous and non-ferrous metal production.

Argon

A key component of stainless steel refining, argon helps to prevent oxidation of molten steel as is used as a stirring agent.

Nitrogen

A carrier and purge gas in steel production, nitrogen is used to prevent oxidation and can be used in the heat-treating process.

Hydrogen

Hydrogen is used to prevent oxydation and is the key component in the heat treating process.

Applications  

INDUSTRIAL APPLICATIONS FOR THE METAL PRODUCTION INDUSTRY

  • Share
  • Print

Argon Oxygen Decarburization

Over 75% of the world’s stainless steel is made with our Argon Oxygen Decarburization (AOD) process. Lower your material costs, produce cleaner metals and increase productivity with AOD.

Direct Oxygen Injection

Used in foundries, mini mills and stainless steel mills, our joint venture partner, Praxair, Inc.'s CoJet® gas injection and Supersonic direct injection processes lower costs and improve furnace productivity.

Oxygen-Enhanced Combustion

Our Dilute Oxygen Combustion system helps convert your furnace air-fuel burners into oxy-fuel burners — increasing productivity, and decreasing fuel consumption, operating costs and emissions.

Heat Treating

From annealing and brazing to sintering and carburizing, our nitrogen and hydrogen-based heat-treating processes are low in cost, high in productivity, and help your operators precisely meet specifications.

Hot Oxygen Technology

Our hot oxygen system rapidly burns hard to combust materials and easily preheats oxygen to high temperatures at point of use.

Inerting

Our Laminar Barrier Inerting technology reduces alloy oxidation, slag formation, and dissolved oxygen, nitrogen, and hydrogen in metals to produce better fluidity, easier casting and fewer flaws.

Stove Oxygen Enrichment

Generate higher blast temperatures without burning more fuel. With our Stove Oxygen Enrichment application, we can help you reduce or potentially eliminate the need for fuels like coke oven gas and natural gas.
