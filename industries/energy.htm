Related Gases  

INDUSTRIAL GASES FOR THE ENERGY INDUSTRY

  • Share
  • Print

Nitrogen

As an inert industrial gas, nitrogen is used as a blanketing agent to separate sensitive products and processes from air. It is also used as a purging agent in piping and equipment to prevent contamination.

Oxygen

Utilizing oxygen in the place of air can increase performance and capital efficiency in many industries. It is often used in boilers and process heaters, industrial fermenters and gasification processes to improve productivity.

Hydrogen

Hydrogen is the energy source in fuel cells. It is also used in biofuel and chemical processing, and can improve utility generator-cooling performance.

Carbon Dioxide

Carbon dioxide is used as a reactant in several biological processes for the production of important chemical building blocks.

Applications  

INDUSTRIAL APPLICATIONS FOR THE ENERGY INDUSTRY

  • Share
  • Print

Oxygen-Enhanced Combustion

Oxygen-Enhanced Combustion applications can help lower NOx emissions in your industrial boilers, furnaces and fired heaters by up to 70%. And when burning high-moisture high-ash fuels, this technology can increase output to nameplate capacity, while stabilizing and improving flame performance. Regardless of the application, Oxygen-Enhanced Combustion is designed to help you generate cleaner power, faster.

Nitrogen Stripping

Remove harmful dissolved gases from your process fluids with our nitrogen stripping application. Together we can tailor a system that will meet your purity requirements.

Direct Oxygen Injection

Direct Oxygen Injection technology improves oxygen transfer rates, production capacity and productivity. It also reduces foaming and flooding in your oxygen-limited aerobic fermentation and propagation reactors.

VOC Treatment & Recovery

Using liquid nitrogen as an indirect refrigerant, this application can condense Volatile Organic Compounds (VOCs) in your process streams, returning valuable products back to your business while utilizing the nitrogen for purging and blanketing operations.

Oxygen for Gasification

With years of experience designing, building and operating commercial air separation units, Airtec has the technology and world-class engineering talent required to deliver the most efficient, reliable and competitively-priced oxygen supply for your gasification needs.

Hydrogen for Fuel Cells

We’ve been providing hydrogen fuel and related technical support to fuel-cell developers and vehicle fleets for years. And as the technologies become even more widespread, we are even better suited to supply the hydrogen and fueling systems your facility requires.
